About

My name is Gergana and I am a web developer. I graduated my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science recently (July 2017) and I am currently looking for the right job to start my career. The technologies I am best at are: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, ASP.NET, MySQL, Adobe Photoshop. I have basic knowledge of several others, like Java, PHP, Perl, XML, Prolog. My high school profile was “System programming”, but I wanted to become a web developer even before that.

I remember being 12 and programming, without actually knowing what programming is. I made a web forum for a German band I used to like. It was HTML-based and you could change everything about it, if you go through the code. I didn’t understand that code and I didn’t know what it was called, but simple logic led me to figuring things out on my own. I figured < b > corresponded to bold, “font size” was the size of the font (duh) and “img” should be the place to store your image. I had that forum for 2 years and changed the appearance of it very often. I played with new tags each day and soon I could understand the whole thing. Later I realized that I was programming in HTML before even knowing what HTML is. This was a very special moment for me, I decided I want to continue writing code.

Jezve arcade game website

Used technologies (for the developement of the website): HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Adobe Photoshop.

This arcade game is a prototype I built for a subject at the university in 2015. The subject was called "Project management" and it was mostly learning about different project management methodologies, like Scrum and Kanban. I was required to build the foundation of an imaginary app or game, as an end course project, and then write the documentation for it. This documentation included project budget, methodology that I think will be best, milestones, risk management, a list of possible problems and complications, required skills and programmers and so on. The project got an Excellent score and the highest mark in the course. The concept is simillar to the game "SpongeBob SquarePants Dinner Dash" and the graphics I made with Adobe Photoshop and some Google images.
